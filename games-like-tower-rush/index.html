Aviator (Spribe): The Category Leader

Aviator by Spribe helped define the instant win genre and has by far the biggest player base. Aviator adds its own community features like live bets and chat. Tower Rush, by contrast, keeps its own tower-climbing style, so try each in the demo to see which you prefer.

JetX (SmartSoft)

JetX is a jet-themed instant win game by SmartSoft Gaming. The mechanic is familiar: bet, watch the multiplier rise, cash out before the jet explodes. It's widely available and, in many versions, supports two bets per round. RTP and max multiplier vary by operator, so check in-game.

Aviatrix

Aviatrix is an instant win game known for its high level of customisation and a strong following in the crypto-casino space. It plays like other instant win titles but leans into personalisation and community. Confirm RTP and availability at your operator.

Spaceman (Pragmatic Play)

Spaceman by Pragmatic Play uses an astronaut theme and keeps the same instant win mechanic: a multiplier rises and you cash out before it ends. It comes from a major provider and is widely available, which makes it an easy alternative to find.
