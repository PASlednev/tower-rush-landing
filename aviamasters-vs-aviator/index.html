Gameplay Differences

The mechanic is nearly identical: bet, watch a plane and a rising multiplier, cash out before the round ends. The differences are in presentation and extras. Aviator leans on its large live community (you can see other players' bets and cash-outs), while Aviamasters focuses on the core game with two-bet play and auto-cash-out. Day to day, the feel is more similar than different.

RTP & Volatility Compared

Both games run at exactly 97% RTP, so each keeps a 3% house edge. They differ in ceiling: Aviamasters is a low-volatility game capped at a x250 multiplier, while Aviator has no fixed multiplier cap and is limited instead by a $10,000 max win per bet. A difference of a fraction of a percent has no meaningful effect on a single session, because every round is independent and random. Don't pick based on RTP alone.
