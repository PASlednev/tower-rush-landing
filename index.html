What Is Tower Rush?

Tower Rush is a real-money instant risk-progression game developed by Galaxsys and listed on galaxsys.co as an 'Instant Game'. Instead of reels or cards, you climb a tower one level at a time, selecting a position on each level. Each successful level increases your multiplier; you can cash out your winnings after any successful level, and a wrong choice ends the round. The appeal is speed and a single, clear decision each level.

How the Cash-Out Mechanic Works

Each round starts after you set a stake. As you climb, each successful level raises the multiplier, and your potential payout is your stake times the current multiplier. You can cash out after any successful level. The outcome of each position is random and unknown in advance, so a wrong pick can end the round at any level. That uncertainty is what makes Tower Rush high variance.

RTP & Volatility

RTP is a long-run average measured across many thousands of rounds; it does not predict any single session, which can swing well above or below it. Tower Rush Playing Styles & Bankroll

There is no strategy that beats Tower Rush. The RTP is fixed and every round is random, so no betting pattern or 'system' changes your odds over time. What you can control is risk. Low-risk style: cash out early at small multipliers (around 1.2x-1.5x) for frequent, smaller wins. Balanced style: aim for mid-range multipliers and accept more swings. High-risk style: chase big multipliers, knowing most rounds will end first. Whichever you choose, set a budget and a loss limit before you start, keep each stake small relative to your bankroll, and stop when you hit your target or your limit. No 'hack', 'signal' or 'predictor' can guarantee a profit.
