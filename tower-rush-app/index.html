Is There a Tower Rush App?

Tower Rush is played through the browser rather than as a standalone app. Because it is an instant, browser-based game, the mobile browser version is the main way to play on a phone or tablet. Some licensed operators package their games inside their own app, so a Tower Rush title may appear there too, but that depends entirely on the operator. Whichever route you use, confirm the source is a licensed operator that lists Galaxsys as the provider before you play.

Playing Tower Rush on Mobile

On a phone the game uses touch controls: you tap a position on the current tower level, and tap the cash-out button when you want to secure your winnings. The interface is responsive, so the tower levels, the current multiplier and the controls scale to smaller screens and stay readable. Rounds are quick and the layout suits portrait orientation for one-handed play, while landscape can give the tower a little more room. The mechanic is identical to desktop: you climb by selecting positions, and each successful level raises your multiplier.

How to Play Tower Rush on Android and iPhone

Getting started on mobile takes a few steps:

  1. Open a compatible mobile browser, such as Chrome or Safari.
  2. Go to a licensed operator that offers Tower Rush and open the game.
  3. Place your stake using the bet controls.
  4. Choose a position on each tower level to climb higher.
  5. Cash out after a successful level, or keep climbing for a higher multiplier at higher risk. A wrong choice ends the round.

Adding Tower Rush to Your Home Screen

You can add Tower Rush to your home screen so it opens like an app icon, straight into the operator's page, without installing anything.

Android

In Chrome, open the operator page with Tower Rush, tap the menu (three dots), then choose Add to Home Screen and confirm.

iPhone

In Safari, open the operator page with Tower Rush, tap the Share button, then choose Add to Home Screen and confirm.
