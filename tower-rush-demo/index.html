Play the Tower Rush Demo

Start the demo, climb the tower one level at a time by picking a position on each level, and cash out after any successful level as your multiplier grows. Because the demo uses virtual credits, you can experiment with different cash-out points without risking real money.

What the Tower Rush Demo Is

The demo is the same Tower Rush game with one difference: it runs on virtual credits instead of real money. How to Play the Demo

Load Tower Rush at a licensed operator that offers Galaxsys games and switch to demo or play-for-fun mode. Set a virtual stake, start the round, and climb the tower by selecting a position on each level. Each successful level increases your multiplier, and a wrong pick ends the round, so cash out after a successful level to lock in your winnings. Repeat a few rounds to get a feel for how the levels and multiplier work. You can also use the play-for-fun demo embedded above.

Demo vs Real Money

The gameplay is identical, but the stakes are not. In the demo, wins and losses are virtual and reset when you reload. In real play at a licensed operator, you can win real money but can also lose your stake, and outcomes remain random. Moving from demo to real play does not change the odds.
