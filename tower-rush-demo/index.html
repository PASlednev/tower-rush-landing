Play the Tower Rush Demo

Start the demo and watch the tower build as the multiplier rises, then cash out before the tower crashes. Because the demo uses virtual credits, you can safely practise your cash-out timing and experiment with different cash-out points without risking real money.

What the Tower Rush Demo Is

The demo is the same Tower Rush game with one difference: it runs on virtual credits instead of real money. The tower, the rising multiplier, and the cash-out button all behave exactly as in real play, so it is the safest way to learn the game. Because no real money is staked, the demo cannot pay real winnings.

How to Play the Demo

Load Tower Rush at a licensed operator that offers Galaxsys games and switch to demo or play-for-fun mode. Set a virtual stake, start the round, and watch the tower build as the multiplier climbs. Press cash out before it crashes. Repeat a few rounds to get a feel for how fast the multiplier moves. You can also use the play-for-fun demo embedded above.

Demo vs Real Money

The gameplay is identical, but the stakes are not. In the demo, wins and losses are virtual and reset when you reload. In real play at a licensed operator, you can win real money but can also lose your stake, and outcomes remain random. Moving from demo to real play does not change the odds.
